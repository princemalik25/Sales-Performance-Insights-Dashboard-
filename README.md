# Amazon Sales Performance & Insights Dashboard (2022)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Amazon sales data to uncover **business performance, customer behavior, and operational risks**.  
The dashboard was built in **Power BI** with a focus on **storytelling, recruiter-ready visuals, and actionable insights**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track **overall sales performance** (orders, revenue, cancellations).
- Identify **high-risk categories** and **order buckets** driving revenue leakage.
- Highlight **geographic trends** in revenue and cancellations.
- Compare **repeat vs new customer behavior** to assess loyalty.
- Provide **prescriptive insights**: *Where is the problem? What’s the solution?*

---

## 📊 Key Features
- **Top KPIs**: Total Orders, Total Revenue, Avg Order Value, Cancellation Rate, Revenue Lost.
- **Category Analysis**:
  - Combo chart: Revenue Lost vs Cancellation Rate by Category.
  - Stacked bar: Delivered vs Cancelled Revenue by Category.
  - Scatter plot: Revenue vs Cancellation Rate by Category.
- **Customer Behavior**:
  - Histogram of Order Amounts.
  - Repeat vs New Customers (Donut chart).
  - Avg Order Value trend by month.
- **Geographic Analysis**:
  - KPI cards: Highest Revenue State, Highest Cancellation State, State with Most Revenue Lost.
  - Map: State-wise Revenue & Cancellation Rate.
  - Bar chart: Revenue Lost by State.
  - Scatter plot: Revenue vs Cancellation Rate by State.


---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Power BI**: Dashboard design, DAX measures, storytelling visuals.
- **SQL / Python (pandas)**: Data cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory analysis.
- **Excel**: Initial dataset exploration and validation.

---

## 📈 Insights Delivered
- **Sets & Kurtas** generate the highest revenue but also suffer the most cancellations → *risky categories*.  
- **Low-value orders (₹0–500)** have the highest cancellation rate → *operational inefficiency*.  
- **Metro states (Delhi NCR, Maharashtra)** drive revenue but also account for the largest revenue loss → *focus area for courier reliability*.  
- **Repeat customers** show more stable buying patterns → *loyalty programs can reduce cancellations*.  

---

## 🚀 Storytelling Angle
The dashboard is structured as a **consulting-style case study**:
- **Overview** → Headline KPIs.  
- **Category Analysis** → Which products leak revenue.  
- **Customer Behavior** → Who buys, cancels, and repeats.  
- **Geography Analysis** → Where revenue comes from and where it leaks.
